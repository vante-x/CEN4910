Advice: What to look for while searching for affordable housing

Travel Costs

Daily travel fees can quickly add up. Paying $3 a day for toll roads will result in $60 a month only for traveling to and from work.

Utilities

Not all housing utilizes the same amount of energy. Older housing is usually less insulated, resulting in more power needed to cool your home.

Rent

Cut down on rent as much as possible. A $100 or $200 difference between two housing options may seem trivial at first but this can result in an additional $2400 over the course of a year.
